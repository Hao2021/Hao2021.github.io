Compound

Author: Sunhao Date: April 17, 2021  12:48:23

The only way to build something that is substantial is through the compounding effect. It can happen both ways it is either the compouding is working for your or against you. Realizing this you should align yourself
to alter the compound effect to serve your best interest.

We, humans, are bad with big numbers and long-term thinking (At least that is the case for me). In hidgesight we know that the micro decision that we take every day will have some sort of effect on us but we failed to see the the huge and tremendous effect of it in the long run.

Reading a single page of book will not have that much effect on our knowledge pool but yet deep down we knew that it is the right thing to do. Now say you still read a single page but you do this every day for a year time you will probably finish around 2 book.

This does not seem much but taking into the consideration that majority of the people on this planet does not managed to finish a single book in a year.
Now, this is the cool part !!!. If you manage to read a single page per day for a few months it will become habitual making reading easier. Your reading skill will be better making you want to read more.

This does not just apply to reading but can be applied to anything you want to improve upon as long you put in the effort and kepp showing up every single day.
