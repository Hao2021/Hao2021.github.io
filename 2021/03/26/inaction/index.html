Action is the antidote for inaction

Author: Sunhao Date: March 26, 2021  11:03:21

There will time when you will feel like a badass and there is also time when you will feel like shit.
You will find your self stuck inside a rut from time to time and it just seem impossible to avoid this.

It is really important that you can acknowledge that you will not feel good 100 percent of the time because
that is how we are designed to be. If we were to be happy and satisfy all the time then there will not be any
advancement in the human evolution and the progression of the civilization in general. If you think about it
there will not be light bulb if Edison is perfectly satisfied with the traditonal oil lamp. The Wright brother
wont create a flying machine if they are constantly satisfied with there daily printng job.

If you are stuck in a self sabotaging and unproductive loop, It is probably a sign that something needed to be
changed. You need to identify what is it that is sabotaging you and take action as soon as possible with the
least amount of effort necessary. Human is a resilient species we are made and designed to endure the
uncomfortable because the ability to endure and adapt is very important for our survival as a species.

The ability to positively cope with the unfavorable emotions and circumstance will serve you well in life.
The best strategy to positively cope is to take action that align with your intention.
