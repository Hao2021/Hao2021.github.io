So good

Author: Sunhao Date: March 27, 2021  10:21:53

I don`t mean that they are trying to avoid being good at stuff but simply people are avoiding the doing
the work especially the uncomfortable work that is required to get good at something.

We often wonder if there is any better solution to everything. People are always in search for this magical
life hack, The magical pills, The magical moment, The get rich quick scheme. There is something about it
that seem very novel at time. Somehow deep down we all know that to do anything great in life you need to
sacrafise. There is no such thing as nothing for something that is why it is really important to make the
sacrafise, take responsibility and bargain with the future.

It is all about the process and making rational decision that serve your intention. Putting in the your work
days in an day out, Pause and look around and cherish the people that is sharing the same path. Be consistent
and persist well in the bad time.

All you need is a strong sense of purpose and clarity of vision to get to the place you wanted to be.
All you need is your north star that shine extremely bright and will guide you through the unknown.
